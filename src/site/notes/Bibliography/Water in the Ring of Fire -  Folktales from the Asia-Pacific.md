	The ring of fire emerged when Sea began to run around, as Sky did to quench his desires, his longing for not being able to touch Sea. As Sea formed mountains and valleys and rivers and streams, Sky was overcome and began to weep.
	
	“Tears were the way by which Sea had brought herself closer to him and the experience of tears was how she had made him understand her true nature. He blinked, and tears of joy fell down as rain to the earth.”
